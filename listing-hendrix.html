About This Property

This unique two-story townhouse in Northeast Albuquerque offers a one-of-a-kind layout with a dramatic spiral staircase, soaring ceilings, and abundant natural light throughout. The home features two covered outdoor spaces including a large private deck and an upper-level balcony with views toward the Sandia Mountains. Inside, you'll find tile flooring throughout, a gas fireplace in the living room, an open kitchen with a pass-through bar, and two well-sized bathrooms with updated lighting. The attached one-car garage provides convenient direct access. A rare find at this price point in 87111.

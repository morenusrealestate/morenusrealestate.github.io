Our Story

Built on Trust, Grown Through Referrals

Morenus Real Estate was founded with a simple idea: real estate should feel personal. Too often, buying, selling, or renting a home feels like dealing with a faceless company. We wanted to build something different — a small, dedicated team that actually knows the Albuquerque market and treats every client relationship as a long-term one.

Today, most of our business comes from referrals — past clients who trusted us with one of the biggest decisions of their lives and sent their friends and family our way. That trust is something we take seriously with every transaction, big or small.
